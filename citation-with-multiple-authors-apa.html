The following rules for handling works by a single author or multiple authors apply to all apa style references in your reference list regardless of the type of work book.

Place citations with no date first followed by works with dates in chronological order.

For example you would write smith clark lane 2018. When citing multiple works parenthetically place the citations in alphabetical order separating them with semicolons.
